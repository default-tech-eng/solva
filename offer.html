Good to know

How do I take Solva?

Two capsules daily, with or without food. Each bottle holds 60 capsules — a 30-day supply.

What's actually in it?

Six actives per serving: Cinnamon Bark 70mg, White Mulberry Leaf 60mg, Juniper Extract 55mg, Bitter Melon Extract 50mg, Berberine HCL Extract 25mg, and Chromium Picolinate 200mcg. Other ingredients: Gelatin, Brown Rice Flour, Silicon Dioxide.

Can I return it?

Yes — unopened products can be returned within 30 days of delivery, in original packaging with proof of purchase. Refunds are issued within 14 business days of inspection.

Where do you ship?

Across the European Union. Orders are processed within one business day; estimated delivery is 3–6 business days after dispatch.

Should anyone avoid it?

If you are pregnant, nursing, taking medication or managing a medical condition, consult your doctor before use. Keep out of reach of children.
